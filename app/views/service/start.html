You'll need:

  • the company number
  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account, which is separate to your WebFiling account

You cannot use this service if:

  • you are an insolvency practitioner acting on behalf of a company (alternative process - email from a valid address)
  • the officer is disqualified
  • the officer is bankrupt
